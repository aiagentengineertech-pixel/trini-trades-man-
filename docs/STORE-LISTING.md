## Permissions — justification (Play + Apple review)
- **Location** (`when in use`) — match customers with nearby jobs/tradesmen and show distance. No background location.
- **Camera** — take a profile/portfolio/job photo.
- **Photos / media** — choose an existing photo to upload.
- Each is requested **only when the user takes that action**, and is covered in the privacy policy.

## Google Play — App content declarations
- **Target audience:** 18+ (adults). Not designed for children → not in Families program.

- **Data deletion:** in-app (Settings → Delete account) and web (`/delete-account`).
- **Financial features:** none in-app today; payments via a licensed processor (see below).

## Payments policy (important)
- Paying a tradesman is a **real-world service** → exempt from Google Play Billing / Apple IAP;
  use the external processor (WiPay). This is allowed.
- The **premium business-suite subscription** unlocks in-app features → on iOS/Android this may
  be classed as digital and require Play Billing / Apple IAP. Safest at launch: keep the suite
  **free**, or confirm the billing requirement before charging in-app.
